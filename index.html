About Us

A Safe Haven for Medically Complex Children

StartCare PPEC (Prescribed Pediatric Extended Care) is a licensed healthcare facility dedicated to providing comprehensive medical and developmental services for children with complex medical needs.

Our center offers a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children receive skilled nursing care, therapeutic services, and age-appropriate developmental activities — all under the supervision of qualified healthcare professionals.

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to grow, learn, and thrive. Our multidisciplinary team works closely with families and physicians to create individualized care plans that address each child's unique medical, developmental, and emotional needs.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Care Services

Our multidisciplinary team provides a wide range of specialized pediatric services tailored to each child's unique needs.

Skilled Nursing

Round-the-clock skilled nursing care provided by licensed registered nurses and licensed practical nurses, including medication administration, vital signs monitoring, and complex medical procedures.

Physical Therapy

Individualized physical therapy programs designed to improve mobility, strength, balance, and motor function, helping children reach their maximum physical potential.

Speech Therapy

Comprehensive speech-language pathology services to enhance communication, swallowing, and feeding skills through evidence-based therapeutic techniques.

Respiratory Therapy

Specialized respiratory care including airway management, ventilator support, nebulizer treatments, and continuous respiratory monitoring by certified therapists.

Developmental Activities

Age-appropriate educational and developmental programs designed to stimulate cognitive growth, social interaction, and sensory development in a fun, engaging environment.

Occupational Therapy

Targeted occupational therapy to help children develop essential daily living skills, fine motor coordination, and sensory processing abilities for greater independence.
